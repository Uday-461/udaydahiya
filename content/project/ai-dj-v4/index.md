## About AI DJ v4

AI DJ v4 is an intelligent music curation system powered by large language models. It combines music metadata with LLM capabilities to create dynamic, context-aware playlists that go beyond traditional recommendation algorithms.

The system analyzes musical characteristics, mood, tempo, and genre relationships to generate cohesive listening experiences. Whether you need a workout playlist, background music for focus sessions, or something to match a specific vibe, AI DJ understands the nuances of music selection in ways traditional systems can't.

## Why I Built This

Music recommendation is typically handled by collaborative filtering and user behavior patterns. But there's so much more context to great playlist curation—the emotional arc of songs, how they transition between each other, the narrative flow of a listening experience. LLMs bring semantic understanding to these dimensions, enabling a more thoughtful and intentional approach to music discovery.

This project explores the intersection of music, AI, and user experience—turning abstract musical concepts into tangible playlists that people actually want to listen to.
